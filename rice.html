Risibisi Rice

Description

Risibisi refers to rice and peas. Stable food for many countries but traditionally it's enjoyed during the festive of st.Mark

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup fresh peas
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt and pepperfor taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the vegetable broth and keep it warm over low heat
  2. In a separete large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent.
  3. Add the rice to the skillet and stir to coat the rice with the oil, onions, and garlic. Cook for a couple of minutes, stirring frequently, until the rice starts to turn translucent.
  4. Add the peas to the skillet and stir to combine with the rice and onions.
  5. Pour in the broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Once it's boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 18-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.
  6. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and season with salt and pepper to taste.
